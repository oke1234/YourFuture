# Privacy Policy – YF. Your Future

Last updated: 2026

YF. Your Future (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the app.

---

## Information We Collect

YF. Your Future does not require users to create an account to use the app.

The app collects and processes the following data solely to provide its core functionality:

- **User-generated content**, such as tasks, goals, streaks, and chat messages
- **Identifiers**, such as an internal user identifier used to associate data with a user or device
- **Diagnostic data**, including crash reports and performance data, used to maintain app stability and reliability

The app does not collect data for advertising, marketing, or tracking purposes.

---

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used only to:

- Provide and maintain app functionality
- Synchronize data in real time
- Enable communication features
- Diagnose crashes and improve performance

Data is not sold to third parties.

---

## Data Storage and Processing

Data may be stored and processed securely using third-party services (such as Firebase) to enable real-time synchronization, messaging, and diagnostics.

These services process data only as necessary to provide their functionality.

---

## User Control

Users control the content they create within the app.  
Tasks, goals, streaks, and chat messages can be modified or deleted from within the app.

---

## Children’s Privacy

YF. Your Future is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
